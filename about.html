About Us

Entrust is about identifying and funding projects that bring transformation to individuals and communities in the developing world amongst the poor and oppressed.Sopporting for blinds, handicaps, orphans Childrens, oldage homes Our donors are individuals, Trusts, small business owners and corporations that are motivated by the opportunity to make a financial contribution to a project that will improve the lives of people in the community where our implementing partners live and serve. We enable our donors fulfill their social and philanthropic goals through partnering with us. We work closely with in-country implementing partners who identify, manage and report on our projects. These partners and projects are spread across fifteen under-developed nations and Australia. We cover the costs of doing this from our own resources, so 100% of donor’s money goes directly to the project!

Child Foundation is an international charity organization that helps children living in poverty remain in school.
Our Vision:
Losing one’s birth family and being placed in an orphanage, regardless of the age it occurs, is traumatic. Scientists have now confirmed that trauma does indeed change a person’s brain neurochemistry. These changes can cause a host of difficulties for the child. They include: Difficulty with emotions (expressing them, identifying them, regulating them), being easily overwhelmed, hyper-vigilance, overwhelming anxiety, disassociation, volatility (easily set-off, rages), hyperactivity, impaired cognition and reasoning skills, depression, sense of hopelessness
Our Mission:
Currently, there are more than 163 million orphans throughout the world, and due to extreme neglect, abandonment, immense poverty, hunger, and lack of education and care, every day another child is at risk of being added to that number
